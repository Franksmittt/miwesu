## ADDITIONAL NOTES FOR AI IMAGE GENERATION

### Location-Specific Details:
- **Location**: Makoppa district, Thabazimbi, Limpopo, South Africa
- **Biome**: Arid Sweet Bushveld (Sweetveld)
- **Vegetation**: Sweetveld grasses (remain nutritious year-round), Acacia trees (flat-topped), Leadwood trees (Combretum imberbe - ancient, gnarled), various bushveld trees
- **Terrain**: Rolling hills, granite koppies (ancient rock formations), red earth, dust-red tracks
- **Wildlife**: Greater Kudu, Blue Wildebeest, Impala, Gemsbok, Warthog, various bird species
- **Climate**: Dry season (winter) - May to August for most images, clear skies, minimal rain
- **Lighting**: Golden hour (sunrise 6-8 AM, sunset 4-6 PM) for most images
- **Road**: D1432 district road - red earth, gravel, winds through bushveld
- **Coordinates**: -24.4523956, 27.0450853
- **Distance**: Approximately 40km from Thabazimbi town

### Style Guidelines:
- **Photography Style**: Professional wildlife/conservation photography, National Geographic quality
- **Color Palette**: Warm earth tones, golden amber, rich greens, browns, red earth tones
- **Mood**: Luxurious, serene, respectful, professional, authentic African bushveld
- **Composition**: Cinematic, well-composed, following rule of thirds
- **Atmosphere**: Exclusive, wild, untouched, conservation-focused

### Important Considerations:
- **No Violence**: All hunting/conservation harvest images should emphasize skill, respect, and conservation - not violence or death
- **Ethical Representation**: Show animals in their natural habitat, healthy and respected
- **Luxury Positioning**: Images should convey exclusivity and luxury while remaining authentic
- **Conservation Focus**: Emphasize conservation, sustainability, and respect for the ecosystem
- **Family-Friendly**: Ensure all images are appropriate for family audiences
- **Authenticity**: Images should reflect the actual Makoppa district terrain and vegetation
- **Professional Standards**: All images should meet professional photography standards
