## Image Specifications

**General Requirements:**
- Format: High-resolution JPEG or PNG
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 for hero images, 4:3 for gallery images, varies for specific uses
- Resolution: Minimum 1920x1080 for hero, 1200x900 for gallery
- Style: Professional, luxury, authentic African bushveld
- Color Palette: Warm earth tones, golden hour lighting, rich greens and browns
- Location Context: Makoppa district, Thabazimbi, Limpopo, South Africa - Arid Sweet Bushveld biome
