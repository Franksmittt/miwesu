The Provenance

Grounded in
Precious Earth

Miwesu stands upon the Penge Formation, a geological rarity dating back 2.5 billion years. This is not merely land; it is a monument to deep time.

The iron-rich soil—stained with the rust of the earth's first breath—offers a grounding rarely found in the modern world.
